Combos

Combos are sequences of moves that, when executed correctly, can deal significant damage to your opponent. They require precise timing and execution.

Choosing which combo to use at which time is very important. However, you also need to check your resources at the same time.
You need to know that the time given to confirm which combo you’re going to use is less than 10 frames. At the same time, you must constantly watch your Drive Gauge. Each player has six bars of Drive Gauge. However, some combos may require you to spend two bars of Drive Gauge. At that moment, you’ll need to consume your resources in exchange for greater damage or a favorable situation. But when your Drive Gauge runs out — because aside from when you actively use it, when an opponent attacks you, you also need to use Drive Gauge to block — once it’s depleted, you will fall into a major disadvantage. This disadvantage could allow the enemy to turn the tables in a single move.
